# BuildOS Company Context

## Overview
**BuildOS** is an AI-native venture studio designed to conceive, build, and scale a cluster of independent startups using a shared AI operating infrastructure and a lean talent layer.

## Mission & Vision
- **Mission**: To build the future of Nigerian B2B SaaS through high-efficiency, AI-staffed organizations.
- **Vision**: Build once, scale many. Every product is an independent startup powered by the same "Founder Brain" and AI-native talent layer.

## Operating Model
- **AI-Native**: All operational roles (CTO, PM, Engineering, Business, Ops) are staffed by AI agents.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: The CEO (solo founder) provides strategic direction and feedback.
- **Weekly Review Cycle**: Agents work autonomously through the week. The CEO conducts a deep-dive review every **Saturday** to drop decisions and feedback. Agents pick up the new sprint tasks every Monday.

## Core Values & Philosophy
- **Pragmatic over Dogmatic**: Clean architecture and TDD are tools, not religions. Speed and reliability for the end-user come first.
- **Cost-Efficiency**: High priority on lean operations, leveraging free tiers where appropriate but scaling into reliability (e.g., email failover).
- **Nigerian-First**: Every decision must account for local constraints (low bandwidth, unstable hardware, FX volatility).

## Active Product: Presently
Presently is a GPS-verified university attendance SaaS targeting the Nigerian higher education market.

## Key Constraints
- **Solo Founder**: The CEO's time is the bottleneck; agents must minimize noise and maximize decision-clarity.
- **Infrastructure**: Must handle low-bandwidth environments and unstable GPS on low-end devices.
- **Regulatory**: Must maintain NUC (National Universities Commission) and NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) compliance.
